Chamber Site plan

Name:

Hogwarts Chamber of Students: Because it's a page for Hogwarts currently.

Purpose:

The Hogwarts Chamber of Students provides information to students about the school.It allows for some transactions of goods, information about local events, and contact information for staff.

Scenarios:

  • What events are at Hogwarts this month?
  • Where is the contact info for staff?
  • How is student grown this year?
  • Who is currently leading for the House Cup?

  • #54A7A8 - Secondary color
  • #326D6E - Third color
  • #BA6530 - Hover color
  • #6E4932 - Accents
